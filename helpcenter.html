Help Center

What is BakeGenuis.ai?

BakeGenuis.ai is an AI-powered tool that transforms vague recipe measurements into precise, gram-based values for better baking results.

How do I convert a recipe?

To convert a recipe, navigate to the 'Convert Recipe' page, enter your ingredients and their measurements, and the AI will provide a precise conversion.

Is this service free?

Yes, BakeGenuis.ai is currently free to use for all users.
